Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Better Golf League Tracker (the "Service") collects, how it's used, and who it's shared with.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: for individual user accounts, your name and email address. For league-based (shared-password) access, no personal account information is required.
  • League data: player names, scores, handicaps, schedules, and other information entered by a League Admin or members to operate their league.
  • Payment information: subscription payments are processed entirely by Stripe. We never receive or store your card number — only a subscription status and Stripe's own customer/subscription identifiers.
  • Usage data: basic technical data (such as pages visited) generated by using the Service.

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to operate and maintain the Service, process subscription payments, communicate with you about your account, and measure the effectiveness of our advertising.

3. Advertising and Analytics

We use, or plan to use, Google Ads conversion tracking (and/or Google Analytics) to understand how visitors find the Service and to measure ad performance. These tools may set cookies or use similar technology in your browser. Google's use of this data is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. We do not use this data to identify you individually outside of aggregate advertising performance metrics.

4. Third Parties We Share Data With

  • Stripe — payment processing. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Google — advertising and analytics, as described above.
  • Hosting/infrastructure providers (e.g. Render, our database provider) — to run the Service itself.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Cookies

We use a session cookie required for logging in and staying logged in. Advertising/analytics tools described above may set their own cookies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain league and account data for as long as the league remains active. You can request deletion of your account or league's data by contacting us below.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for league administrators and adult members and is not directed at children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time; the "Last updated" date above will reflect the most recent change.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy, or requests to access/delete your data, can be sent to CustomAdditiveEngineering@gmail.com.
