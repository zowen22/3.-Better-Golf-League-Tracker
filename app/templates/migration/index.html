players.csv required
Expected columns:

teams.csv optional
Expected columns:
team_name, player1, player2

schedule.csv optional
Expected columns:
week, date, home_team, away_team
date format: YYYY-MM-DD or MM/DD/YYYY
scores.csv optional

date, player, hole_1 … hole_9

CSV format tips

Column headers are case-insensitive and common aliases are recognised automatically. You can export from GLT by logging in → Reports → Export Roster and Export Scores, then uploading the downloaded files here.

If a player already exists in your league (matched by first + last name), they will be linked rather than duplicated. Scores can only be imported into an existing season or a new season you create at the confirmation step.
