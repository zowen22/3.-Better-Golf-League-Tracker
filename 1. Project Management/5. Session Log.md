## 2026-06-13 - iOS Auto-Advance: Root Cause Found + Fixed

*Status: `COMPLETED`*
*Type: `Manual`*
*Linked Audit: `Audits/2026-06-12-score-entry-audit.md` (P0-5 — resolved)*

### What Was Done
